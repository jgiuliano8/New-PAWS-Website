Investors Bank Fundraising

Investors Bank, which has recently merged with Citizens Bank, has a program entitled, Care2Share, where the bank makes a donation of .25% annually based upon a members eligible bank account balance to an enrolled charity organization. PAWS has joined this program so we are an enrolled charity organization. We need people who currently have eligible accounts at Investors/Citizens bank to enroll in the Care2Share Program or to open an account with Investors/Citizens, and then join the Care2Share program, and then select PAWS. An Eligible account is a personal checking account or a business checking account. It costs the bank account holder nothing - The bank is making the donation based on the balances of those account holders who enrolled in the program and who selected us as their charity. It is a donation from the bank. See Investors Bank for more details.
